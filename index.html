Fast, light and functional front-end framework

And it haz a picture of a cat

Increase conversion by decreasing page load time to 2" on slow mobile networks. Improve development speed by using standard building blocks that are flexible to customise and combine. Improve stability and compatibility by not pushing the browsers any more than necessary. The negligible weight of 9 KB removes the barrier between your content and your customers everywhere.

✓ Please do

  • Use a grid with arbitrary cell elements inside .row. Cell classes like .column23 respected, but not required.
  • Use vertical alignment in cells, it's finally easy and dependable.
  • Embed any kind of element into any container-type element: Cell, List, Slider, Accordion.

The grid is simpler and doesn't insist on the famous 12 columns. Just add a <div> to a .row. Column width classes, like .column23, are optional. Wrap embedded .row with .embed-row to keep alignment.
